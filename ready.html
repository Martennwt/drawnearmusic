Good News
The greatest gift, told simply

Are you readyfor the good news?

This page is about the Gospel: the message, the “Good News”, maybe the best news you will ever be told. It has been passed from hand to hand for over two thousand years, and most people have never heard it told plainly. It truly could change your life. But only if you read it openly.

An honest word, before you read anything

Whatever you expect to find here: first, forget everything you think you know about church and religion. The buildings, the rules, the pointed fingers. This page is about none of that. It is about the gospel.

And do you know what “gospel” actually means?

No mystery, no church word. It comes from Old English: gōd spel, literally a good telling, good news to pass on. And “Evangelium”, the word half the world uses? Ancient Greek: eu-angelion. Same meaning, older language: good news. That is all these words have ever meant.

Which leaves one question: which good news?

That is the only thing this page exists to answer.

✦

Read it only when you can read it open. Open means: you can imagine, just for a few minutes, that there might be a truth out there that is less comfortable than the one you carry today, or believe you carry. You are allowed to reject it afterwards, completely. Saying no to something you have honestly heard is a fair answer. But closing the door before you have looked, like with everything in life, is rarely the best idea. At least you will have heard it once.

And what happens after you read it? That is yours alone. Whether this message bears fruit, whether the seed grows, no one can decide for you. It depends on one thing only: whether you are honestly interested in what is true. Historically true, and true in what the gospel itself says. Nothing here will push you. This is the good news about Jesus as the New Testament tells it, explained so simply, and maybe so eye-opening, as you have never heard it before.

You will have questions. Good ones, hard ones. Put them aside, just for now. They all get their turn later. When you are truly ready, you can read the good news right here, or even listen to it as an audiobook in different languages (both coming soon). And if today is not the day: go back to the music, or take the quiet check-in, give yourself time. Nobody is counting. Just do not read this page with a closed heart. It is about the greatest gift ever handed to anyone, and a gift cannot be received with closed hands.
